Racial Bonds

A statistical analysis of pre-trial detention & bail bond practices in Connecticut

The problem

In Connecticut state correctional facilities, Black people always make up the largest proportion of the pre-trial inmate population. In 2016, White people made up the next largest proportion, but by 2020 White and Latinx people were incarcerated in roughly equal numbers.
